# Ebrora

Professional Excel templates built specifically for UK construction and civil engineering.

## About

Ebrora provides ready-to-use spreadsheets designed for site managers, civil engineers, and project teams working to CDM 2015 and British Standards. Our templates cover HSE & Safety, Project Management, Asset & MEICA Tracking, Wastewater & Utilities, and Cost & Carbon Calculations.
