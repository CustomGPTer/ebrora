Description

The Temporary Works Register is a comprehensive Excel template that provides full compliance with BS 5975:2019 — the code of practice for temporary works procedures and the permissible stress design of falsework. It ensures that every temporary works item — from scaffolding and propping to cofferdams, excavation support, and temporary bridges — is formally registered, designed, checked, approved, inspected, loaded, and eventually dismantled through a controlled, auditable process.

The permit to load management module ensures that no temporary works item is loaded until the design has been checked, the TWC has approved the installation, and all pre-loading inspections have been completed and signed off.

The RAG-rated dashboard provides an instant overview of every temporary works item on the project, colour-coded by lifecycle stage and compliance status.

✓ Full BS 5975:2019 compliance for temporary works management procedures
